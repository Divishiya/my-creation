Specialty tapes are designed to meet the precise demands of industries that require high-performance adhesive solutions for excessive situations and specialized programs. Polyimide Tape

APolyimide tapes, also called Kapton tapes, are renowned for their remarkable thermal balance and electric insulation properties. Ideal for high-temperature covering, circuit board insulation, and aerospace packages, these tapes perform seamlessly in excessively warm conditions and harsh conditions. As trusted ST 0104 Polyimide Tape Manufacturers in India, we ensure top-quality products that meet the highest requirements.

Variants:

  • Polymide Tape 1 mil (Kapton Tape)
  • Polymide Tape 2 mil (Kapton Tape)
  • Polymide Tape With Liner 2 mil (Kapton Tape)
  • Double Side Polymide Tape 0.095mm (Kapton Tape)

Aluminum Foil Tape

Aluminum Foil Tapes are valued for their excellent conformability, reflective surface, and heat conductivity. Coated with pressure-sensitive adhesive, they resist chemicals and vapors, making them ideal for sealing and insulation in demanding applications.
